Visitor Analysis

The audience for this site is most likely low income students in their early 20's. When using the site the visior is most likely preparing a presentation for homeowrk or class in their apartment with their laptop. However, they may be looking for images to use in their presentation on their mobile device anywhere. The visitor is trying to find a specific image that they can use and keep for free. Time has no affect on the site, so if the visitor has procrastanated a project and are completing it in the wee hours of the morning, that is perfectly aceptable for the site!
