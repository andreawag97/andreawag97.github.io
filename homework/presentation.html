Alignment

What is Alignment?

Alignment is the arrangement of elements in a straight line or the correct relative positions. It is such an integral part of website design that some people might not realize that it is even there. We make decisions about alignment by deciding how and where elements are placed on the page.

Why is Alignment Important?

  • Helps craft stronger and more compelling compositions.
  • Makes things more visually appealing and less confusing to look at.
  • Can create a better user experience.
