Site Purpose

This site’s purpose is to inform the intended audience of the weather forecast for their city in a simplistic and effective manner.

Audience

The target audience for this site are those who live within the cities listed on the weather site. As well as those who play sports outside, those who love fashion, those who are travelling, photographers, and many more who have a desire to know the current weather forecast within their city. The age range on this target audience can be any where from 17-70, as checking the weather is a common activity for those who do anything outside. This site should include a mobile friendly version since most of the audience most likely use their smart phone to quickly check the weather.
